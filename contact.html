Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Join Now" button on our homepage, fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to go.

How do I withdraw my winnings?

You can withdraw your winnings by going to your account dashboard, clicking on "Withdraw", and following the instructions. We support various payment methods including UPI, bank transfer, and digital wallets.

Is fantasy cricket legal in India?

Yes, fantasy cricket is legal in India as it is considered a game of skill rather than a game of chance. However, some states have their own regulations, so please check your local laws.

How long does it take to process withdrawals?

Most withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours. However, during peak times or for larger amounts, it may take up to 3-5 business days.
