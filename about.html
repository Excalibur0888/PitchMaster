About PitchMaster

Learn more about India's premier fantasy cricket platform

Our Story

PitchMaster was founded in 2018 by a group of cricket enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform where fans could engage with the sport they love in a more interactive and rewarding way.

What started as a small project has now grown into one of India's most trusted fantasy cricket platforms, with millions of users across the country.

Our mission is to enhance the cricket viewing experience by allowing fans to create their own dream teams and compete with friends and other cricket enthusiasts.
