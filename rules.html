Fantasy Cricket Rules

Understanding how to play and win

How Fantasy Cricket Works

Fantasy cricket is a game where participants assemble virtual teams of real cricket players and score points based on those players' actual statistical performance in real matches.

At PitchMaster, we've created an exciting platform that allows cricket fans to test their knowledge and strategy while competing for amazing point rewards.

Whether you're a cricket expert or just getting started, our comprehensive rules will help you understand how to play and maximize your chances of winning.

1. Team Creation

Team Composition

Each fantasy team must consist of 11 players including:

  • 1-4 Wicket-keepers
  • 3-6 Batsmen
  • 1-4 All-rounders
  • 3-6 Bowlers

You must select players from both teams playing in the match.

Captain and Vice-Captain

You must select one player as your team's Captain and one as Vice-Captain:

  • Captain earns 2x points
  • Vice-Captain earns 1.5x points

Choose these roles strategically as they can significantly impact your total score.

Credit System

Each player is assigned a credit value based on their past performance and current form. You have a total of 100 credits to build your team, so choose wisely!

Top players will cost more credits, requiring you to balance star players with value picks.

3. Contest Types

1

Head-to-Head Contests

Compete directly against one opponent. The higher-scoring team wins the entire point pool.

2

Leagues

Join contests with multiple participants. Points are distributed based on your rank in the final standings.

3

Mega Contests

Large-scale contests with thousands of participants and significant point pools. These contests typically have a tiered point reward structure.

4

Practice Contests

Free contests that allow you to practice your skills without risking any points.

4. Important Rules

Team Modification

You can modify your team until the deadline (typically the match start time). After the deadline, no changes are allowed.

Make sure to check player availability and match conditions before finalizing your team.

Multiple Teams

You can create multiple teams for the same match, but each team must be unique and can only be entered once per contest.

Creating multiple teams allows you to try different strategies and increase your chances of winning.

Cancelled/Abandoned Matches

If a match is cancelled or abandoned, all related contests will be cancelled and entry fees refunded.

In case of Duckworth-Lewis method being applied, points will be awarded based on the official result.

Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old to participate in paid contests. Fantasy cricket may be restricted in some states - please check your local laws.

PitchMaster reserves the right to verify your identity and location before processing withdrawals.

Fair Play Policy

At PitchMaster, we are committed to maintaining a fair and enjoyable environment for all users. We have a strict fair play policy that all players must adhere to.

No Multiple Accounts

Creating or using multiple accounts is strictly prohibited. Each user may only have one account on the platform.

No Collusion

Collusion between players to gain an unfair advantage is not allowed. This includes team sharing and coordinated contest entries.

No Automation

Using automated scripts, bots, or any software to create teams or enter contests is prohibited.

Fair Play Commitment

Violation of our fair play policy may result in disqualification from contests, forfeiture of winnings, and/or permanent suspension from the platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first fantasy cricket team?

To create your first team, select an upcoming match, choose 11 players within the 100 credit limit, select a captain and vice-captain, and then enter a contest. Make sure to follow the team composition rules (1-4 wicket-keepers, 3-6 batsmen, 1-4 all-rounders, and 3-6 bowlers).

Can I change my team after the match starts?

No, you cannot make any changes to your team after the match starts. All team selections must be finalized before the official match start time.

How are points calculated in case of a rain-affected match?

In rain-affected matches where the Duckworth-Lewis method is applied, points are awarded based on the official statistics of the match. If a match is completely abandoned without any play, all related contests are cancelled and entry fees are refunded.

When are winnings credited to my account?

Winnings are typically credited to your account within 1-2 hours after the match result is officially declared. For tournaments spanning multiple days, winnings are credited after the completion of the entire tournament.
